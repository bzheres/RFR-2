About Us

Welcome to Ready For Radiology, a brand new, up-and-coming, online resource for radiology education. Founded by a passionate medical student, this platform is dedicated to providing users high-quality educational materials to help revise anatomy, radiology physics, and image interpretation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower aspiring radiologists with the knowledge needed to start preparing for a career in this exciting area of medicine. We strive to make radiology education accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone, even those who are not interested in radiology! We would love for others with a passion for radiology, anatomy, or physics, to also get involved. If you want to contribute through either writing an article or providing contructive feedback, we would love to hear from you!

Founder

I am a fourth-year medical student, based in Sydney, Australia, with a passion for the field of radiology. This passion led to the creation of "Ready for Radiology", where I aim to share my journey trying to become a radiologist with others by teaching anatomy, physics, and the basics of image interpretation. One day I hope to be a specialised radiologist in Australia, and as I work towards this, I hope I can inspire and teach others along the way.

Our Values

  • Attention to Detail

    Radiologists need to be excellent at paying attention to detail. We recognise this and strive to provide detailed articles sharing the information you need to know while prepaing for radiology exams.

  • Keeping it Simple

    Radiology, and the physics that come along with it, can become overwhelming. While providing all the information we believe you need, we aim to present content that is still easy to understand to help minimise the stress of studying.

  • Improvement

    We aim to continuously improve in everything we do, from how our content is structured, to the resources we use, to our interaction with our community. This involves you! Get involved and write articles yourself, ask questions, and lets continue to improve together.

Our History

We currently have a very short history, being founded in 2024. However, we plan to continue providing educational content and hope to grow into one of your go-to resources.
