Recommended Resources

There are likely thousands of resources one could use to learn radiology and to stay up to date with important events. However, here I have listed out a few of my personal favourites. As a disclaimer, I am not associated with any of the following, and these are all based of my personal experience. So if you feel like I have missed an important resource that people should know about, let me know!

Books

Here are some of the best textbooks for radiology education:

  • Learning Radiology: Radiology for Medical Students by X. Author
  • Title: Fundamentals of Radiology by Y. Author
  • Title: Radiographic Anatomy by Z. Author

Websites

These websites provide excellent online resources for radiology education:

  • Radiopaedia - A free online radiology resource with a vast library of radiology cases and articles.
  • Learning Radiology - A comprehensive site for learning radiology, including quizzes and educational videos.
  • Aunt Minnie - A radiology community site providing news, cases, and resources.

Online Courses

Consider these online courses to further your radiology education:

  • Medical Imaging: Principles and Applications - Coursera
  • Introduction to Radiology - edX
  • Diagnostic Imaging in Medicine - FutureLearn

Journals

Stay updated with the latest research by following these radiology journals:

  • Radiology - The leading journal in radiology research.
  • American Journal of Roentgenology (AJR) - A prominent journal in diagnostic radiology.
  • Clinical Radiology - A journal that publishes high-quality articles on all aspects of diagnostic imaging.

Community and Forums

Engage with the radiology community through these forums and social media groups:

  • Reddit Radiology - A community for discussions and sharing resources related to radiology.
  • Radiology Residency on Facebook - A group for radiology residents and professionals to share experiences and knowledge.
  • Doximity - A professional network for physicians, including radiologists.
